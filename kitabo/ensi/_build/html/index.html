Immutable#

The neural network we describe, interwoven with themes of mythology, philosophy, and economics, becomes a map of human ambition, frailty, and technological evolution. It begins with an input layer grouped into three symbolic pairs: Theomachy, Mortals, and Fire. These pairs echo primal struggles: divine conflict, human mortality, and the Promethean gift of technology. Each group serves as a node, compressing the chaotic vastness of human history into legible patterns.

The Input Layer: Foundation of Conflict and Energy#

At the first layer, Theomachy represents cosmic and systemic entropy—struggles that echo divine warfare. This is not just mythology; it captures systems bound by tension, whether ecological or economic. Mortals, caught in the Red Queen Hypothesis, perpetually race to keep up, doomed to eventual frailty. Fire symbolizes the technological Promethean spark, a gift meant to elevate mortals above their natural limitations. Yet, fire is also the double-edged sword of energy, consumption, and hubris.

In the context of Blankman Freed, the actions of bailing out rivals and clients simultaneously represent attempts to play god—a mortal wielding the Fire to control outcomes beyond their natural reach. The incoherence of his strategy, however, defies the logic of the Red Queen. Mortals are meant to let others fail, for competition and attrition are part of the evolutionary algorithm. The failure to embody this principle suggests ulterior motives, cartel-like consolidation, or an illogical dance with power.

The Hidden Layer: Compression of Dynamics#

As the network compresses the input into the hidden layer, it distills these struggles into deeper abstractions. Theomachy filters into perceptions of “Cosmos-Entropy,” balancing chaos and order. Mortals are compressed into “Life-Needs” and “Ecosystem-Costs,” reflecting the frailty and resource dependency intrinsic to their survival. Fire becomes “Generative-Means” and “Cartel-Ends,” embodying technology’s dual role: fostering creativity or enforcing monopolistic hierarchies.

This hidden layer is a crucible of contradictions. For Blankman Freed, the compression reveals a deeper tension: his simultaneous creation of liquidity (Generative-Means) and consolidation of power (Cartel-Ends). The hidden layer captures his attempts to transcend the Red Queen Hypothesis, aspiring not just to survive but to dominate. Yet, the system resists such monarchic aspirations. It is inherently unstable when one mortal wields fire meant for many.

The Generative Layer: Explosive Outputs#

From the compression, the generative layer emerges as a field of dynamics. Here, the hidden abstractions are weaponized as ratios, tokenized into systems of exchange, and monopolized by those with asymmetric power. In this layer, technology and economy converge. Blankman Freed’s actions—bailing out rivals and clients—become tokens of systemic distortion. His interventions disrupt the natural attrition expected in a competitive market, fostering an unnatural hierarchy.

This layer’s tension lies in its cyclical feedback: actions that initially stabilize (bailouts) may destabilize the larger system. Promethean fire here becomes less a gift and more a weapon, wielded not to benefit humanity but to consolidate power. The Red Queen looms as a reminder that systems driven by such monopolization are inherently unsustainable. They falter under their own weight.

The Output Layer: Values and Vulnerabilities#

At the final layer, the network outputs a synthesis of its inputs and compressions. This is where the generative dynamics manifest as societal values and vulnerabilities. Promethean fire, if wielded judiciously, can lead to “Exuberant Jubilee” or “Freedom-Dance in Chains,” where technology liberates while binding us to new dependencies. Conversely, when monopolized, it results in “Stable-Conservative” stagnation or “Unveiled Resentment,” as systems buckle under inequity.

Blankman Freed’s saga, through this lens, reveals the fragility of a system that leans too heavily on artificial stabilization. His actions, meant to secure power, may instead lead to revolutionary volatility. The fire that once burned to sustain his empire risks consuming it.

Iteration: A New Input Layer#

The output layer feeds back into the next iteration of the input layer, creating a recursive cycle. The failures and vulnerabilities of the previous generation become the foundational struggles of the next. Theomachy evolves into new cosmic challenges—entropy on a planetary or even interstellar scale. Mortals adapt their Red Queen race, incorporating lessons from the past. Fire, as technology, becomes even more refined, potent, and dangerous.

The cycle continues, each iteration layering new complexities onto the old. Blankman Freed, in this view, becomes a cautionary node in the broader network—a mortal who tried to transcend his nature but was ultimately consumed by the very fire he sought to wield.

import numpy as np
import matplotlib.pyplot as plt
import networkx as nx

# Define the neural network fractal
def define_layers():
    return {
        'World': ['Cosmos-Entropy', 'Planet-Tempered', 'Life-Needs', 'Ecosystem-Costs', 'Generative-Means', 'Cartel-Ends', ], # Theomarchy, Mortals, Fire
        'Perception': ['Perception-Ledger'], # God
        'Agency': ['Open-Nomiddleman', 'Closed-Trusted'], # Evil & Good
        'Generative': ['Ratio-Weaponized', 'Competition-Tokenized', 'Odds-Monopolized'], # Dynamics, Compromises
        'Physical': ['Volatile-Revolutionary', 'Unveiled-Resentment',  'Freedom-Dance in Chains', 'Exuberant-Jubilee', 'Stable-Conservative'] # Values
    }

# Assign colors to nodes
def assign_colors():
    color_map = {
        'yellow': ['Perception-Ledger'], # , Judgement Day, Key
        'paleturquoise': ['Cartel-Ends', 'Closed-Trusted', 'Odds-Monopolized', 'Stable-Conservative'], # Slavery, Colonialism, Worker
        'lightgreen': ['Generative-Means', 'Competition-Tokenized', 'Exuberant-Jubilee', 'Freedom-Dance in Chains', 'Unveiled-Resentment'], # Das Kapital, Frankenstein, AI
        'lightsalmon': [
            'Life-Needs', 'Ecosystem-Costs', 'Open-Nomiddleman', # Ecosystem = Red Queen = Prometheus = Sacrifice
            'Ratio-Weaponized', 'Volatile-Revolutionary'
        ],
    }
    return {node: color for color, nodes in color_map.items() for node in nodes}

# Calculate positions for nodes
def calculate_positions(layer, x_offset):
    y_positions = np.linspace(-len(layer) / 2, len(layer) / 2, len(layer))
    return [(x_offset, y) for y in y_positions]

# Create and visualize the neural network graph
def visualize_nn():
    layers = define_layers()
    colors = assign_colors()
    G = nx.DiGraph()
    pos = {}
    node_colors = []

    # Add nodes and assign positions
    for i, (layer_name, nodes) in enumerate(layers.items()):
        positions = calculate_positions(nodes, x_offset=i * 2)
        for node, position in zip(nodes, positions):
            G.add_node(node, layer=layer_name)
            pos[node] = position
            node_colors.append(colors.get(node, 'lightgray'))  # Default color fallback

    # Add edges (automated for consecutive layers)
    layer_names = list(layers.keys())
    for i in range(len(layer_names) - 1):
        source_layer, target_layer = layer_names[i], layer_names[i + 1]
        for source in layers[source_layer]:
            for target in layers[target_layer]:
                G.add_edge(source, target)

    # Draw the graph
    plt.figure(figsize=(12, 8))
    nx.draw(
        G, pos, with_labels=True, node_color=node_colors, edge_color='gray',
        node_size=3000, font_size=9, connectionstyle="arc3,rad=0.2"
    )
    plt.title("Frankenstein", fontsize=15)
    plt.show()

# Run the visualization
visualize_nn()
